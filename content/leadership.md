### Jin-Hyoung Kwon
**CEO / Founder**
Mr. Kwon founded the aio in 2011. Prior to the aio, he served as a Marketing & Strategic Director and worked as a Manager in the Product Planning Team of **Samsung Electronics’ Memory division**. He planned and developed new types of memory such as Fusion Memory and successfully launched One-DRAM for Samsung Galaxy S1.

### Sang-Yul Baek
**CFO / Co-Founder**
Mr. Baek is a co-founder of the aio. Prior to the aio, he served as a product planner in the Product Planning Team of **Samsung Electronics’ memory division**. He is one of the starting members of the Samsung SSD project and holds an **MBA from MIT Sloan**.

### Sei-Jin Kim
**Executive Vice President, Marketing**
Mr. Kim previously held senior leadership roles at **Samsung Electronics**, including SVP of the Mobile Business Division and VP of the Memory Semiconductor Division.

### Han-Joo NA
**Executive Vice President, P&P Center**
Previously held Vice President roles at **SK Hynix** where he developed SSD and Mobile Solution products. Prior to SK Hynix, he was a Vice President of Memory Technology at **Unigen** in the United States.

### Seung-Hyun Han
**CTO / Co-Founder**
As one of the co-founders, Mr. Han has developed SD Card & eMMC Flash Solution Firmware as a Project leader since 2006 at **Samsung Electronics’ Memory division**. He also co-founded **Gamevil**, a leading mobile game company.

### Young-Sik Koh
**Senior Managing Director, R&D SoC Development**
Prior to the aio, he served as a SoC development Director of **SK Hynix** and worked as a member of the Smart Card IC development team of **Samsung Electronics**.
