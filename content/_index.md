## Core Technologies

Our technological leadership is built on three pillars:

### High Performance
*   **NAND Parallel Control**: Maximizing throughput through multi-channel expertise.
*   **Distributed Request Queuing**: Optimized command handling for low latency.
*   **Dynamic Block Allocation**: Efficient endurance management.

### High Reliability
*   **Reliability Guaranteed Flash Engine**: Superior error correction (LDPC) and endurance.
*   **Advanced FTL**: State-of-the-art Flash Translation Layer with bad block management.
*   **Sudden Power Off Recovery (SPOR)**: Data integrity preservation in critical conditions.

### Low Power
*   **Deep Sleep Modes**: Minimized power consumption during idle states.
*   **Voltage Scaling**: Optimal power delivery for varying performance requirements.
*   **Efficient Architecture**: Optimized instruction sets for specialized flash operations.
